Zeiyara is a comprehensive fitness and social platform that combines AI-powered training plans, social video sharing, and interactive virtual coach chat. The app helps users create personalized workout routines based on their fitness goals and intensity preferences. Users can choose between virtual male and female coaches according to their personal preferences, ensuring a comfortable and personalized training experience. Users can browse workout videos from the community, interact with their selected virtual fitness coaches, and manage their training history. With an intuitive interface and intelligent AI assistance, Zeiyara makes fitness training accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone.
